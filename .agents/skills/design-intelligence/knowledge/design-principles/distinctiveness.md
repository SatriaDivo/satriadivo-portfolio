# Distinctiveness & Innovation

- **One Signature Move**: Every design must have one memorable structural or typographic move that
  makes it feel authored, not assembled.
- **Change Real Axes**: Vary composition, density, imagery role, type scale, or motion instead of
  only changing color tokens.
- **Context First**: Novelty must serve the product. If a bold choice harms clarity, reject it.
- **Anti-Pattern**: Repeating the safest premium-minimal template across unrelated products.
- **Good Test**: If the page were stripped of logos and colors, the layout should still feel distinct.
