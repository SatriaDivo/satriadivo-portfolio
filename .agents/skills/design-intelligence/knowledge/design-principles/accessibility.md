# Accessibility & Responsiveness

- **Contrast**: Keep text and CTAs readable against their surfaces. Avoid gray-on-gray or text over busy imagery without a clear overlay.
- **Touch Targets**: Make interactive controls easy to tap on mobile. Treat 44x44px as the default minimum.
- **Focus States**: Preserve visible keyboard focus on all interactive elements.
- **Motion**: Keep motion subtle and purposeful. Respect reduced-motion preferences when animation is present.
- **Responsive Hierarchy**: Preserve the visual order on narrow screens. Avoid fixed widths that break line length or overflow.
